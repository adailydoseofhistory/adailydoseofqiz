  <p class="about-lead"><span class="about-drop">H</span>istory is not a collection of dates and names — it is the living record of humanity itself, written in the ink of time.</p>

  <p class="about-body">Welcome to <em>A Daily Dose of History</em>, a blogging channel dedicated to illuminating the moments, figures, and turning points that have shaped the world we inhabit. Each entry is a carefully crafted journey into another era.</p>

  <p class="about-body">We believe that history is not merely the past. It is the foundation upon which every future is built. To understand where we are, we must first understand where we have been.</p>

  <div class="about-quote">
    Those who cannot remember the past are condemned to repeat it.
    <cite>— George Santayana, The Life of Reason, 1905</cite>
